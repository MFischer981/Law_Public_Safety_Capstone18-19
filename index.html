WestMEC's two-year Law, Public Safety, & Security program explores the basic skills used in patrol functions, defensive tactics, use of force and first aid. Students will also explore the concepts of constitutional law, forensic science, first aid / CPR, and more.

West-MEC's Law, Public Safety, & Security program prepares students to seek industry certification or licensure in their field of study.
